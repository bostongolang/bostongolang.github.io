Our speakers this month:

Ron Evans and Adrian Zankich

Introducing Gobot (http://gobot.io), a new robotics framework written in Go. Gobot can communicate with many different kinds of hardware devices, and integrate them together. With surprisingly few lines of code, you can write interesting applications that tie together Arduinos, ARDrones, Spheros, and more... even all at the same time! Gobot is based on Go, giving it the ability to support the levels of concurrency that are required for industrial-strength robotic applications. Gobot has a built in API-server, debugging console, and even shows you how to do Test-Driven Robotics (TDR).
